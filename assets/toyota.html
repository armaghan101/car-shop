Digital use:

  • HEX. Hexadecimal codes are a six-character shorthand for their equivalent RGB values. HEX codes are often the preferred notation for web and HTML.
  • RGB: RGB stands for Red, Green and Blue, the colours of light combined on screens to display the digital colour spectrum. RGB values are interchangeable with HEX codes.

Print use:

  • CMYK. CMYK stands for Cyan, Magenta, Yellow and Key (black), the four standard inks mixed during the printing process. CMYK should be used for all standard print applications.
  • PMS. PMS stands for Pantone Matching System. PMS colours are a single premixed ink colour, which allows brand colours to be printed with total accuracy, no matter the printer.
